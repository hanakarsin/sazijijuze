Osun State Governorship Election 2022

May 2024 · 3 minute read

The Osun State governorship election kicks off today with many residents coming out in their numbers to elect the leader to pilot the affairs of the State for the next four years.

Naija News reports that the ruling All Progressives Congress (APC) is working to retain the state for another four years and prevent parties like the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), Labour Party, Accord Party and others from winning.

Governor Gboyega Oyetola of the APC is up against Senator Ademola Adeleke of the PDP, Rt Hon. Yusuf Lasun of the Labour Party and Akin Ogunbiyi of Accord Party.

Stick with Naija News as we bring you update on the electoral process Osun State…

Update: Most of the registered voters in PU16, Ward 1, Isale Osun, Osogbo LGA have already cast their votes. The electoral officers and the electorate are waiting for the exercise to end.

Update: Voting has ended in PU 11, Ward 04, Ila-Orangun, but some voters are waiting to monitor the sorting and counting of the ballots.

Update: Voters told TheCable that party agents gave them ₦5,000 each while some others said they received ₦3,000 at PUs 2 and 7, Bode-Osi Ward 8, Olaoluwa LGA.

12:23: Minister for Interior, Rauf Aregbesola is yet to cast his vote at PU 1, Ward 8, Ilesha East

US consulate election observers are on ground at PU 004, Ward 04, Oba Laoye Grammer School, Ede South.

Yusuf Lasun, candidate of Labour Party, speaks after casting his vote at PU 04, Ward 5, Woru compound, Irepodun LGA.

11:00: Gboyega Oyetola, governor of the state and APC candidate, has just cast his vote alongside his wife, Kafayat.

Exercise going on smoothly at PU 6, Isare Ward 8, Ilesha East.

Voting has commenced in the Osun Governorship election, with electorates trooping out in their numbers to vote for their preferred governorship candidate.

Meanwhile, operatives of the Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC) are currently on the ground in Osun State to curb vote buying during the governorship election in the state today (Saturday).

The operatives of the anti-graft agency arrived at the Freedom Park, Old Garage, Osogbo, the state capital around 8 am.
