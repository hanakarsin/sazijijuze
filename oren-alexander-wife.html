Who Is Oren Alexander's Wife? She Is a Successful Model

March 2024 · 4 minute read

Oren Alexander's Wife Has Already Set Her Instagram to Private — Can You Blame Her?

Oren Alexander's wife is a model who has walked hundreds of runways. Will she be walking out of her husband's life?

By Jennifer Tisdale

Jun. 14 2024, Published 1:09 p.m. ET

The luxury real estate world has become the center of whispers, side eyes, and a metric ton of distancing after twin brothers Oren and Alon Alexander were accused by two women of raping them over a decade ago. Although Alon chose to buck the family business by not going into real estate, Oren and their other brother Tal co-founded the luxury property brokerage Official Partners. At the time of this writing, Oren has been removed from their website and has gone dark on all social media.

Oren is not the only one who is digitally shutting things down. His wife has also gone private on Instagram and has yet to release a statement regarding the shocking allegations. The couple hasn't been married long, which means their honeymoon period has undoubtedly come to a screeching halt. Who is Oren Alexander's wife? She was definitely in the public eye before they got together.

Oren Alexander's wife is a model.

Oren is married to Brazilian-born model Kamila Hansen, who sat down with The Daily Front Row in July 2021 to chat about how she managed to hold strong in the oft-difficult world of being professionally attractive. She was almost 29 years old and had been discovered in Brazil then quickly signed to a modeling agency there.

To be perfectly honest, the interview is fairly superficial and sometimes tough to believe. For example, when asked about misconceptions in the modeling world, Hansen says you don't have to be skinny, tall, or exercise often, but that's certainly not the messaging most people receive in that industry. Make no mistake about it, Hansen still works out, but she doesn't overdo it. "Everybody is different and has different needs," she told the outlet.

The conversation switches to empowerment and the women who have inspired Hansen. "I feel like in general, women are powerful and wonderful creatures, but whenever I’m facing some difficult challenge, I always think about my mom and all the lessons she has taught me when I was a kid," she said.

Hansen left home quite young and immediately began traveling the world when the only language she could speak was Portuguese. Through hard work and determination, she eventually learned how to speak four different languages, but she says that was a difficult time in her life. Perhaps it was the most difficult moment until the accusations about her husband surfaced.

Oren Alexander and Kamila Hansen met in 2018.

As a model, it makes perfect sense for Hansen to have invited Vogue to her wedding. The outlet did a nice spread about the day and even shared how she and Oren met. Hansen was hanging out with gal pals in Las Vegas when Oren walked up to her in a hotel lobby and asked if she was lost. Apparently, that was all it took for Hansen's interest to be piqued. "It was just like that," she said.

Pretty soon, they were in a relationship, and four years later, in August 2022, Oren popped the question while the couple was in Aspen. "It happened on the balcony of the house we were staying in. It was very casual, and surprisingly, I had no idea," she said of the proposal. The wedding took place in Islamorada, Florida in April 2023 and was more of an intimate gathering than a huge blowout.

Though the two were hitched, the event went off without a hitch and included a Jewish ceremony, live music from both of their cultures, and their dog wearing a tuxedo. "It all went as planned, and we wouldn’t have done anything differently," shared Hansen.
