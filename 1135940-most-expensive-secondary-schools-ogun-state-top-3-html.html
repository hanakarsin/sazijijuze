Most expensive secondary schools in Ogun State | Top 3

June 2024 · 4 minute read

Do you know the most expensive secondary schools in Ogun State? Are these schools really the best? The most popular and expensive secondary schools in Nigeria are located in such places like Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt. But some of them can be found in Ogun State. In this post, we have listed the most expensive secondary schools in the state.

It is amazing to find out that there are some secondary schools in Nigeria today with school fees in the figures of millions. You probably wonder where these schools are and how many people can afford them or spend such amounts just on secondary school education.

Some of these expensive secondary schools are regarded as the best schools in Nigeria. For most Nigerian parents looking to give their wards quality education, private schools have been the answer for so many years. But the cost of private school education keeps going up every year. To get quality education you have to be ready to pay for it.

In Nigeria today, there are lots of private schools in a range of prices. The notion is that the more expensive a school is, the better quality of education it provides. On the average, the expensive secondary schools provide the best overall quality of education because of all the opportunities they give their students. Some of the things these schools offer include modern facilities and serene environment, modern technologies, excellent and quality teachers, trips abroad, etc. Let us look at some of the most expensive secondary schools in Ogun State.

The Bells Group of Schools, Ota, Ogun State

School Address - Km 11 Idi-Iroko Road, Ota, Ogun State, Nigeria

School fees - Boarding School -1.2 Million Naira, Day School - 650, 000 Naira

Website - www.thebellsschools.org/secondary/home/

The Bells Group of School was founded in 1991 by the former president of Nigeria, Olusegun Obasanjo. The school includes a comprehensive secondary school, boarding school for boys, boarding school for girls, and a day school. The school environment is state of the art designed for maximum learning and assimilation. The school has well-equipped laboratories, fully equipped ICT laboratory suite technical workshops, 24-hour health care services, standard and hygienic dining facilities, well-equipped classrooms among others. Students are prepared for both local and international examinations.

Corona Secondary School, Agbara, Ogun State

Address – Agbara Estate, Agbara, Yenagoa Road, Ogun state, Nigeria

School fees – 2.25 Million Naira

School website – www.coronaschools.org/css

Corona secondary school is one of the most expensive secondary schools in Ogun State and one of the most expensive in the country. It was established in 1992. It is a co-educational school with modern boarding facilities and a very supportive community.

The school aims at producing students who are excellent and versatile and can fully express themselves and their culture in any environment be it local or international. Corona secondary school uses a unique curriculum which allows all their students to be well prepared for the local exams like NECO, regional exams like WAEC, and the international qualification exams like IGCSE. They also train their students in skills that will help them be lifelong learners. The Corona Schools’ Trust Council focuses on developing world-class schools with awesome teaching methodologies, high standard curriculum, excellent staff quality, modern library, and technology facilities, etc.

Day Waterman College, Abeokuta, Ogun State

Address – Asu Village Road, Abeokuta Shagamu Expressway, Abeokuta, Ogun State

School fee- 3.7 million Naira

Day Waterman College, Abeokuta is probably the most expensive school in Ogun State. It is a modern day boarding school with an excellent and exciting learning environment for children. The environment offers world-class facilities, a peaceful and a focused setting for study. The school is not so popular with many probably because of the school fees.

Some of the things Day Waterman College offers include modern facilities and serene environment, modern methodologies and technology, excellent and quality teachers, trips abroad, etc.

We cannot jump to the conclusion that the most expensive secondary school in Ogun State is the best in the State. But these schools are obviously among the best educational institutions in the country. You do not have to feel bad if you cannot afford the education at these establishments, there are secondary schools with more affordable fees that have proven to be better than the expensive ones. Unfortunately, quite often, expensive schools are more of a show off of the class system in the society.
