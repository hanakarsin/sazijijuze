Career

Burak kicked off her career in the information technology sector where she spent three years before moving to media. She landed a role as a broadcast assistant for BBC weather and used the opportunity to train as a broadcast meteorologist. She began by broadcasting as a relief forecaster for BBC regions and nations.

She was eventually confirmed as a permanent member of BBC East Midlands’ forecast team and later a lunchtime news anchor. In 2006, Burak left BBC for Sky News where she used to present Sky News overnight as from midnight to 0600. Her programmes at the time were Sky News on the Hour (0100-0300) and Sky World News (0300-0600). In 2012, she left Sky News but returned in 2013.
