PHOTOS: Foluke Daramola And Hubby Celebrate Anniversary

April 2024 · 3 minute read

Nollywood actress Foluke Daramola and her husband have been married for 2 years, and yesterday they did the unexpected to celebrate their wedding anniversary!

The couple threw a party at their Omole, Lagos home, treating friends and colleagues to a 2-in-1 event; the wedding anniversary and Foluke's birthday! The Nollywood actress-cum-director was clearly in high spirits and danced to various songs including the popular 'Shoki' song.
