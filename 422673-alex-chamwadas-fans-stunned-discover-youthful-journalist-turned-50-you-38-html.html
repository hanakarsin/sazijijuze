Alex Chamwada's Fans Stunned to Discover Youthful Journalist Turned 50: &quot;You Look 38&quot;

April 2024 · 3 minute read
  • Veteran Kenyan scribe Alex Chamwada marked his birthday while revealing he is now 50
  • Journalist Chamwada expressed gratitude to God for the gift of life
  • Reacting to his post, social media users noted that Chamwada looks much younger than he is

On Monday, August 16, 2021, renowned Kenyan journalist Alex Chamwada celebrated his birthday.

The Chams Media founder shared a photo on social media holding a walking stick, saying it was a gift from a friend in Lodwar.

“Received these gifts from a friend in Lodwar, Michael Peikan, a tour guide as I turned 50,” he wrote.

The scribe expressed gratitude to God for life writing:

Chamwada's father

In July, the journalist shared a photo of himself going on holiday with his mother, and a fan reacting to the image wondered why he had not tagged his father along.

Responding to the netizen, Chamwada shared an emotional story about losing his father to cancer.

“You don’t have to comment on posts like this one just for the sake of it. I doubt that you know our family properly. Our family is more than a single post on social media. Mzee passed on in 2011 after a long struggle with cancer," the journalist stated.

Chamwada added that they walked with his late father throughout his illness, pointing out that he passed away a happy man.

“We don't have to go on rooftops to say everything about ourselves. We are blessed. Don't pain yourself for what you don't know. Hate and negative thinking is not necessary."
