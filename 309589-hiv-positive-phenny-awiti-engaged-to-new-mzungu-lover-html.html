HIV positive Phenny Awiti engaged to new mzungu lover

Kenyan HIV positive mother of two beautiful HIV negative girls Phenny Awiti has finally found her missing rib.

The selfless mum and HIV activist is officially off the market, if a recent photo of her flaunting an engagement ring is anything to go by.

Phenny, on Wednesday, July 3, surprised her online fans after sharing a photo showing off her engagement ring on her official Facebook account.

Accompanying the photo was a nice tell-all caption that clearly indicated the lass had accepted her man's proposal.

''And what a time to be alive It is a "Yes" she wrote.

Following her announcement, countless people thronged her social media pages with endless congratulatory messages.

Being one of our biggest fans, we reached out to her for more details about the milestone.

In a candid interview with TUKO.co.ke, Phenny expressed her joy and narrated how the awesome moment unfolded.

''For now the name is withheld, wedding plans are underway. Actually I got proposed to in a very simple way, not like some fairy tale I see over here,'' she said.

The HIV activist said she was equally surprised as she did not expect her man to go down on one knee that soon.

The lass, who is currently in Brandenburg Gate, Berlin, Germany revealed wedding plans are currently underway but was hesitant to share the identity of her German fiance.

''I was in the house, he called me from the bedroom, he was jn the sitting room, told me that he wanted me to see something, then I was just waking up, in my night dress, and he went down on one knee. And that was it,'' she added.

Phenny grabbed the attention of Kenyans when she came out to share about her journey for the first time.

In earlier reports filed by TUKO.co.ke, Phenny revealed her life took a turn when she was in Form Two at Asumbi Girls' High School in 2008; at that time she was just 16-year-old with no sexual partner and the thought that she could be positive did not ever cross her mind.

The lass who is now involved in many projects made her HIV/Aids status in 2017 on social media and revealed she was born with the disease.

She met a number of men most of whom were not satisfied with the fact that people can be intimate and not get infected.

Phenny finally met the father of her two beautiful girls and like any other man, she disclosed to him her status and he was okay with it at first but later changed his mind, dumping her for another woman.
