List of private colleges in Nairobi

March 2024 · 9 minute read

Private colleges in Nairobi are independent institutions of learning that set their policies and goals and funded privately. These educational centres are smaller than public or private universities but offer approved courses by Kenya’s Ministry of Education and other international regulatory bodies. Although there are many different types of private colleges in the city, many of them specialise in specific areas of study. For example, some offer liberal and fine arts studies, and others dedicate their resources to technology, medical, and business fields.

The advantage of studying at a private college in Nairobi is that you will get the opportunity to customise your programme of study. Since many of these institutions enrol fewer students, you can work closely with advisers and take full advantage of the resources provided.

List of the top private colleges in Nairobi

Although these educational centres have limited options when it comes to areas of study, their courses are a perfect stepping stone to a prosperous professional career. Here is a list of ten private colleges in Nairobi and some of the programmes they offer.
