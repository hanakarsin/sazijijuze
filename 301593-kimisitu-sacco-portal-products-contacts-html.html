Kimisitu Sacco portal, products, and contacts

June 2024 Â· 6 minute read

Kimisitu Sacco is one of the financial institutions in Kenya offering varied financial services to its customers. It began as an initiative of the staff of ICRAF in March 1985. These members wanted a platform that allowed them the opportunity of accumulating their savings, and from which they could get loans for productive projects.

While the Sacco started with a few founders namely: Dennis Wambugu as the chairman, Jane Waweru as the secretary, Charity Kanyeki, Fred Gitau, James Wahome, and Simon Kanani, it grew tremendously from a shared office location in town to what it is today. The potential to form a solid Sacco came about when Kimisitu opened membership to other international organizations including IDRC, Ford Foundation and the Rockefeller Foundation, which were the earliest to join. From then on, the Sacco has grown in leaps.

Kimisitu Sacco portal

The portal is an important part of the entire organization as it is what gives members access especially when transacting online. Going by the Kimisitu Sacco new portal, members have three options of activities to do online. You can either log in as a returning user, or sign up if you are joining as a new user, and finally, you can choose to view old statements directly from the portal. This makes a lot of things convenient. You do not have to visit the offices in person to access some of the basic services you may need as a member.

To log in, you need to submit your member number and password. The Sacco is doing everything possible to ensure that your transaction online is safe.

Kimisitu Sacco products

In case you are scouting for a new Sacco, you may want to know what is on offer. While the Kimisitu new portal login will convince you of any concerns you may have regarding safety and convenience, there is more to look for in terms of services and products. Fortunately, the organization has a number of packages that may be of interest to you.

1. Premier loan

This is the type of loan offered by the Sacco to help you buy off your bank loan at no charge. With a repayment period of 60 months and the freedom to borrow up to 30 million shillings, members can easily sort out their loan issues comfortably. The loan's interest is 1.3% on a reducing balance rate.

2. Member deposits

This allows members to save securely with an opportunity of earning premium dividends on their savings. In case you just want to save and be sure that your money is safe then this is the option you should take. You can always access credit facilities when you save. Make sure that you are a registered member and that you make a minimum monthly contribution of 3000 shillings.

3. Holiday saving account

Are you planning for a holiday? If you want a stress-free one then consider this product and grab it. As long as you are a member of the Sacco, have registered for the account and have submitted your copy of ID, then you can start saving for a minimum of 6 months for your stress-free holiday. The account allows you to withdraw all that you saved and start again. Minimum monthly deposit of 1000 shillings are required, and no charges are exerted on the account.

4. Little Angels saving account

Are you trying to teach your kids some financial literacy and want them to save? If so, this is the account to go for. You can open an account with your child's name and operate it for them. The minimum monthly operating amount is 500 shillings, which is also the minimum monthly contributions. You can get a piggy bank and enjoy interest rates of 7%.

5. Boosting facility

This package boosts your savings to allow you to access the amount of loan you desire. Your savings are boosted by 100% to allow you to access a bigger loan. The boosted amount is eligible for interest earning of 5% annually as a one-off payment. The boost only occurs for amounts not more than 500,000 and is credited to your account.

6. Education loan

This loan helps members further there various academic needs and should be paid within 12 months. The interest is charged at a monthly rate of 1% on reducing balance. It is offered once every week.

7. Special development loan

This loan is offered to members that want to build or renovate their homes. It comes with a payment period of 48 months. Members get three times their savings as long as all other pending loans are deducted, with a maximum of 10 million offered. This loan is available once a month and the interest charged is 1% monthly on reducing balance.

8. Instant loan

This type of loan comes in handy every time there is a need for quick cash. Members of Kimisitu can enjoy this package where one can get a maximum of 400,000 and pay it back in 6 months. The limit will be based on your salary and you only need one guarantor. You will be charged 5% interest per month on reducing balance.

9. Insurance loan

Due to the partnership of Kimisitu with CIC Group, members are advantaged when it comes to insurance loans. You will also enjoy negotiated insurance rates. Other perks include no charges, no guarantors needed, no 2/3 rule application, repayment period of 6 months and waived deposit multiplier requirements.

10. Normal development loan

Whether you want a loan to buy a car, or capital to start a business, or even some money to purchase some valuable asset, the normal development loan comes in handy. The affordable loans are to be paid in 60 months and are offered on the basis of the members' savings. You get three times your savings, minus all outstanding loans.

The above are just but part of the products offered by the Sacco. You can enjoy much more by contacting them through relevant channels. Whether your interest is in the Kimisitu Sacco dividends that you earn when you save, or you are eyeing their lucrative loans, the first step is to make contact.
