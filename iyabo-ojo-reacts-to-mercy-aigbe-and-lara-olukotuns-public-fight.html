Actress Iyabo Ojo Reacts To Mercy Aigbe And Lara Olukotun's Public Fight

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Nollywood actress Iyabo Ojo has reacted to the public fight between her colleague Mercy Aigbe and Lagos Socialite Lara Olukotun.

Naija News earlier reported that Mercy Aigbe, Iyabo Ojo, Lara Olukotun and many others were having fun at an event when the celebrities got into a heated argument which later degenerated into a fight.

The incident reportedly happened on Sunday, June 12, 2022, in the Lekki area of Lagos.

In the video sighted online, Iyabo Ojo who was seen siping a drink from her cup seemed surprised at Lara’s action but couldn’t do anything to help the situation.

Another video also showed the moment some people were seen attempting to separate Mercy Aigbe and Lara Olukotun as they engaged in a physical fight.

Reacting after the videos made the rounds on social media, Iyabo Ojo took to her Instagram page to share photos of herself smiling saying that’s the look she gives knowing she’s doesn’t support violence.

She wrote: “The look you give when you don’t support Vawulance”

Taking to the comment section, a fan hailed Iyabo Ojo for being classy and not influenced by the dirty lifestyle of her colleagues.

Isioma-yocambe wrote: “Iyabo Ojo is just too classy God, make them no go stain her white mobs, the only your actress I love Kai… between anyways, anyways”

Iyabo Ojo responded saying she was on the run.

She wrote: “Na Japa.com I dey.”
