Having a Child with Celebrity is Not a Ticket to Wealth, Muthoni Mukiri

  • YouTuber Muthoni Mukiri advised women only to get kids when they are ready and not because a man is financially stable
  • According to the celebrity, women who think they are trapping men with kids only end up putting themselves in cages
  • She also mentioned that she promised herself never to get kids out of wedlock and her friends in such situations cautioned her too

Media personality Muthoni Mukiri has cautioned women who think having a child with someone influential or wealthy will set them up in life.

Muthoni Mukiri cautions moms-to-be

The YouTuber chided such women and explained most of them are only creating drama for their own lives.

Mukiri said she decided to address the issue after a series of emails from fans who got pregnant with people they thought were everything in life.
