Colleges in Kenya

There are numerous colleges in Kenya offering different types of courses. Some of the most popular colleges though are those offering computer courses from basic to advanced levels. These are spread all over the country with Nairobi carrying the majority of the said colleges.

In case you want to specialize in IT and related courses, it is important to find a college that is approved and recognized by the ministry of education and other relevant authorities. The last thing you want is to spend months pursuing a course in a college that is not established or recognized by any accrediting authority. The following are some of the most common and well known colleges that offer computer courses.

1. Intel College Kenya

This is one of the registered colleges in Kenya. It is one of the reliable colleges in Nairobi offering IT and business courses you can trust. The college is recognized and approved as a higher learning education institute in the country. It has been registered by the ministry of higher education science and technology which makes it one of the most reliable colleges in Kenya. This said, the college has been around for several years since its establishment in 1990. It specializes in offering computer and business courses.

The college is associated with the University of Sutherland from the UK in a collaborative partnership. Quality standards offered at the college are measured and checked by the commission of University education of Kenya as well as related UK authorities. The college is located in a serene environment in Westlands, Nairobi area. It focuses on providing an environment enriched with the latest technologically enhanced learning spaces that foster effective learning.

Students get a chance to study at their own convenience since classes run throughout. The lessons are offered in the evenings, throughout the day and on Saturdays. Furthermore, the college offers courses from basic certificate levels to Masters levels thus accommodating all students that may take an interest in the courses.

3. Institute of Software Technologies (IST)

This college is based in Parklands Nairobi. It was established in 1993 and later registered in 2004. This is one of the colleges in Kenya that comes in handy for IT professionals and business analysts seeking career advancement. The college operates more on a consultancy level with organizations than as an ordinary training school. It partners with different companies in the training of their IT staff.
