Scientific laboratory technicians are supposed to perform necessary examinations concerning chemical, physical or biological fields. Occupying this position means to work in a team, so it is essential to perform your particular job and tasks accurately, in order not to interrupt the complex process of work in the lab.

Moreover, the proper functioning of the lab should be guaranteed, both as a team and working space, as any mistake can put in danger the employees and equipment. That is why following all the instructions and work safety standards is the foremost task of the laboratory technician.

What you are going to work with as a lab technician is a great contribution to the development of the nation's science environment. Your involvement will support the promotion and improvement of contemporary science and medicine of Nigeria. And the impact you will make on it can't be underpriced, as it operates an essential function in the establishment steps of research and development (R&D) and experimental inquiry and study.

The list of duties you will be responsible in the laboratory is quite vast and may vary according to the level of experience and particular position you take throughout the period of your professional development. But here are the main things you are to deal with:

  • Performing lab tests to provide reliable and accurate results for further scientific examinations;
  • Carrying out regular assignments correctly according to the specified instructions to complete inquiries;
  • Preparing examples and specimens;
  • Constructing, maintaining and operating conventional workroom facilities;
  • Keeping facilities in a sanitary and usable condition and ensuring the harmless discharge of waste;
  • Recording and interpreting investigation outcomes to display to senior co-workers;
  • Using workstations and performing mathematical calculations to provide charts and diagrams;
  • Ensuring the lab has all the necessary working materials, checking their availability in stock, expiry dates, and labels.

The specifics of the tasks will vary depending on the establishment you work for. For instance, if the institution is dealing with the water industry, you will be chiefly working with gathering and analysing water samples, and .if your company is dealing with environmental health issues and researches, you might be required to perform analyses with food samples.

Apart from the importance and necessity of the Science Laboratory Technology profession, they are quite attractive due to the salary that can be earned if you work in this sphere. Honestly speaking, the wages may vary depending on some factors like experience and company rate, but still, they are quite reasonable.

According to the website mysalaryscale.com, the average salary of a laboratory technician in Nigerian is about 209,000 Naira. As we mentioned before, its size depends on some aspects. For example, the stated wage of laboratory technician with working experience of a maximum of two years (entry-level) is 171,000 Naira. For specialist with mid-level (working experience from 3 to 9 years) is 88,000 Naira. And senior-level laboratory technicians with experience starting from 10 years can earn up to 1,240,000 Naira.
