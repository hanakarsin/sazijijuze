Emma Hernan Probably Shouldnt Date Anyone Else on Selling Sunset

March 2024 · 4 minute read

Who is Emma Hernan dating today? Emma and Christine Quinn's shared ex is Peter Cornell, who also works at the Oppenheim Group. Learn more.

Fans can't get enough of Netflix's Selling Sunset or its cast — including Emma Hernan. The blond beauty is the CEO of a successful food company called Emma Leigh & Co., a hardworking real estate agent, a former model, and a reality TV star.

She reportedly has a net worth of $3 million, and it doesn’t look like she’s slowing down anytime soon.

Emma's got a lot going on, but what about the romance department? Here’s what to know about Emma Hernan's dating life.

Who is Emma Hernan dating?

Spoiler alert: Spoilers for Season 5 of Selling Sunset below.

In Season 5 of Selling Sunset, Emma started seeing a Texas-based project developer named Micah McDonald. Micah and Emma met when he was her client on the show. Although she wasn’t accustomed to dating someone she worked with (again), she took a chance on him due to their mutual attraction.

“I usually don’t mix business with pleasure, but Micah’s a little bit different,” she said after their first date. “We had like one or two meetings and I was like, ‘Oh my god.’ Not only is he attractive and successful and confident, but we actually have a lot in common."

Emma and Micah dated throughout Season 5 (which filmed in 2021), but their relationship reportedly ended around 2022. That May, the Netflix star confirmed Today that she was “single and dating” and “open to a relationship.” Though she never said if she and Micah were over, it sounds like she is back on the market.

Emma Hernan previously dated Peter Cornell.

Season 4 of Selling Sunset heavily focused on Christine Quinn and Emma Hernan's drama over a shared ex. The handsome gentleman they both previously dated was later identified as Peter Cornell, another real estate agent from the Oppenheim Group. He’s currently the director of the sports and entertainment division for the company.

Emma and Peter reportedly got engaged in 2017 and broke up in 2018 on good terms.

A source told Page Six: "There wasn’t any drama, and it just wasn’t a right fit, but they’ve been friends ever since." The relationship ended because they simply “grew apart.” The breakup reportedly went much smoother than Peter’s split from Christine, which Elite Daily shared happened when Christine spotted him with Emma while they were still together!

While Emma and Peter parted ways romantically, Selling Sunset fans hoped they might reunite. Just three months after Season 4 dropped, Emma and Peter were spotted together on Feb. 24, 2022, as seen in photos obtained by E! News. Immediately, fans wondered if the formerly engaged couple were back together. However, a source confirmed to Us Weekly that the pair were not dating.

Several months after their outing, Peter and Emma proved they were still on good terms when they bought a house together in Los Angeles, Calif. While this isn’t the norm for some former couples, the real estate agents agreed the spot was too good to pass up. Emma confirmed the exes weren’t living in the house together, though they’ve discussed it.

“We bought it as an investment property,” Emma explained to Us Weekly in October 2022. “You know, at some point, there was the thought [of] maybe, will we move in together? Will we not? I think I’m very open about where he and I are and don’t always know. I’m very open about where he and I are.”

Why did Emma Hernan and Peter Cornell break up?

Emma confessed that her and Peter’s love affair might not be over. She also shared in her October 2022 chat with Us Weekly that the exes have a “complicated” relationship and that her engagement to Peter wasn’t “the right timing.”

“Am I going to end up with Peter? I don’t know,” Emma told the outlet. “But right now, it’s just a friendship and a business relationship, and we do a lot of business together. So, if you see us spotted out and about, it doesn’t mean we’re together.”
