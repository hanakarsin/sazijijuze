'Asiwaju Boy' - Yul Edochie Sparks Reactions With President Tinubu's Outfit

May 2024 · 3 minute read

Nollywood actor, Yul Edochie, has sparked reactions online with photos rocking an outfit with the face of the Nigerian President, Bola Ahmed Tinubu.

Naija News report that the thespian took to his Instagram page to declare full support for Tinubu’s administration.

Yul said he is unapologetically a boy to President Tinubu as he believes in him and praying for him to succeed.

He also has urged Nigerians to relax because the former governor of Lagos State will fix Nigeria.

The white outfit has the inscription, “Relax, Jagaban will fix Nigeria”

He also captioned the post, “Asiwaju Boy. 100%. Unapologetically. I love Tinubu @officialABAT. I believe in him, and I’m praying for him to succeed.”
