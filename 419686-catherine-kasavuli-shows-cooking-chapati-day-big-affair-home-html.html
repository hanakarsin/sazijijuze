Nowadays, chapatis do not get the same 'respect' as it used to in many households, but for revered TV girl Catherine Kasavuli, nothing has changed.

In her caption, Kasavuli hilariously said she always gets extra when cooking chapatis, adding that it was still a huge affair in her house.

"Am I the only one who still gets extra whenever l cook chapatis? It's still a big affair in my house. #TudumisheMilaTafadhali," she wrote.
